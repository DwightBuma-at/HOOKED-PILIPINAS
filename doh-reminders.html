Drug Testing Laboratory — Requirements Checklist

Source: hfsrb.doh.gov.ph/drug-testing-laboratory/

DOH HFSRB
Category Requirement Details / Notes
DocumentaryNotarized Application FormDuly accomplished and notarized application for License to Operate (LTO) as drug testing laboratory
DocumentaryDTI / SEC / CDA RegistrationProof of business registration — DTI for sole proprietor, SEC for corporation, CDA for cooperative
DocumentaryMayor's / Business PermitCurrent and valid local government business permit for the operating address
DocumentaryProof of Ownership / LeaseLand title / deed of sale for owned premises, or notarized lease contract (minimum 2 years) for rented
DocumentaryArticles of Incorporation / PartnershipFor corporations and partnerships — SEC-registered articles with latest GIS/AFS
PersonnelHead of LaboratoryLicensed physician or registered medical technologist; valid PRC ID + DOH-approved training certificate in drug testing
PersonnelMedical Technologist / AnalystLicensed MedTech (RMT) with valid PRC ID; must hold a DOH-accredited drug testing training certificate
PersonnelAuthorized Specimen Collector (ASC)Completed DOH/DOLE-accredited training on urine specimen collection; valid ASC certificate
FacilityPermit to Construct (PTC)DOH-HFSRB issued PTC required before any construction, renovation, or modification of the laboratory space
FacilityMinimum Floor AreaFree-standing SDTL: minimum 40 sqm; Hospital-based: minimum 20 sqm. Must be dedicated solely to drug testing
FacilityFunctional Area ZoningMust have clearly defined areas: Waiting/Reception, Specimen Collection (private), Specimen Processing/Analysis, Records Room, Toilet with blued water
FacilitySanitation & SafetyProper ventilation, lighting, biohazard waste disposal system, eyewash station, fire extinguisher, and first aid kit
EquipmentScreening EquipmentDOH-approved immunoassay analyzer or rapid test kits; current calibration certificates required for all instruments
EquipmentConfirmatory EquipmentGC-MS or LC-MS/MS required for Confirmatory DTL; calibration logs and preventive maintenance records
EquipmentSupporting Lab EquipmentCentrifuge, refrigerator (2–8°C), freezer (−20°C), pH meter, pipettes, biohazard containers, and PPE
Quality AssuranceNRL Proficiency TestingMandatory participation in the National Reference Laboratory (NRL) proficiency testing program with satisfactory rating
Quality AssuranceStandard Operating ProceduresDocumented and approved SOPs for specimen collection, analysis, QC, reporting, and result confidentiality
Quality AssuranceQuality Control ManualWritten QC manual including internal controls (positive/negative), reagent lot tracking, and corrective action records
Chain of CustodyCoC Forms & ProceduresDocumented chain of custody from specimen collection to result — signed, dated, and traceable at every step
Chain of CustodySpecimen Collection PrivacyPrivate, well-lit toilet with blue-dyed water; direct observation or monitored collection per DOH protocol
Chain of CustodyRecord KeepingAll drug test records must be kept confidential and retained for at least 2 years per RA 9165 and DOH regulations

Reference: DOH HFSRB — Administrative Order No. 2010-0024 | Republic Act 9165
